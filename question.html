I am aware that these questions are very open; that's exactly the point. They are meant to invite reflection beyond a clear scientific framework. I love discussing them, so feel free to reach out by email. Please do not copy large parts of this page without linking back to me.

Thinking and Perception

  • What shape are your thoughts?
  • Can thoughts be measured or quantified?
  • What would half a thought be?
  • Can we learn to think without putting everything into boxes?
  • How do you recognize people?
  • To what extent do you believe your opinions are more "right" than others'?

Language

  • What is your favorite word in your native language, and do its translations make you feel the same way?
  • Why did German avoid triple consonants before the 1996 spelling reform?
  • What is your favorite Latin poem?

AI and Philosophy

  • Can large language models be sentient?
  • How does this relate to the mind–matter problem — and what's your view on it?

Questions on Questions

  • What is the definition of a question?
  • What makes a good question?

Things we take for granted

  • What is truth?
  • Is the sky really blue?
  • Do you like the concept of "favorite things"?
  • What are today's religions — or their modern replacements?
  • Does everyone have to believe in something?

Questions true mathematicians will answer in a heartbeat

  • What is your favorite prime number?
  • What is your favorite shape?
